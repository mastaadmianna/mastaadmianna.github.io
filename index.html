About My Education.

After clearing 10th board exams, I had science as further studies in my fate and after attempting JEE and failing at it miserably, I landed into a decent college for pursuing my BACHELOR'S DEGREE in telecommunication engineering. Engineering, not interesting me caused bad academic performance and led to an year drop after 2nd year of the programme. I always wanted to explore graphic designing but never could, so having an year for myself, I started discovering things about designing that interested me like nothing ever could. Starting from learning adobe's photoshop and illustrator to exploring animations to futher gaining a deeper interest in ui/ux designing, I set-up my mind to pursue designing as my career by studying further. At a certain period of time during B.E, I gave up to my fate thinking engineering jobs are all I have to settle with, but an academic fallback led me to explore my desire and that in turn firing up a great hope for pursuing what i truly want and I am full of life again. I greatly admire this saying in hindi - "Jo hota hai ache ke liye hota hai".
